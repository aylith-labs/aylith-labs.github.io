## Vision

Mental health is the #1 health concern globally, yet most people can't afford $150-300/session therapy. Selfbloom isn't a chatbot pretending to be a therapist — it's a structured wellness tool with clinical foundations that makes evidence-based support accessible and affordable.

## The Problem

1 in 5 adults experience mental illness yearly, but barriers are everywhere: cost ($150-300/session), access (2-6 week waitlists), quality (existing apps are shallow or gamified without clinical rigor), and stigma. Existing self-assessment tools provide scores without actionable follow-through.

## Key Differentiators

- **Clinical foundations**: Built on validated psychological frameworks (Big Five, CBT protocols), not pop psychology
- **Structured, not freeform**: Guided exercises and assessments rather than open-ended chatbot conversations that can go off-track
- **Growth tracking**: Longitudinal progress visualization so users can see tangible improvement over weeks and months

## Vision

A studio shipping many products runs many things, and they scatter across consoles, dashboards, and tabs. Infra Hub is the control room — one place that aggregates the state of every service and deployment, surfaces health and freshness, and links out to where the detail lives. The answer to "is everything okay" should be a glance, not an investigation.

## The Problem

Operational state fragments the moment you run more than a couple of services. Each tool has its own dashboard, each deployment its own console, and the only way to know the overall state is to open ten tabs and assemble the picture by hand. There is no single pane, so there is no single answer.

## Key Differentiators

- **One pane for the estate**: every service and deployment in a single view.
- **Glanceable state**: health and freshness up front, detail one click away.
- **Aggregates, not replaces**: links out to the real consoles instead of hiding them.
- **Built for the studio**: the central hub for everything Aylith runs.

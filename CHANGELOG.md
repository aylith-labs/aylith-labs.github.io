# Changelog

All notable changes to **aylith.com** — the Aylith studio site: brand, the
method, and a `/projects` catalog of everything the lab has shipped.

Screenshots adapt to your theme (light/dark) and are hosted on
[`media.aylith.com`](https://media.aylith.com/aylith-com/). The site's own brand
marks (logos, lettermark, favicon) live in `landing/static` as functional assets.
Format follows [Keep a Changelog](https://keepachangelog.com/); no tagged releases
yet, so entries are grouped by milestone date.
